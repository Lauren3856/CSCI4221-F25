## 2. UI/UX Designer Role (Your Position)
**Responsible:** *UI/UX Designer*

### Key Responsibilities:
- Design the **layout and structure** of all app screens.
- Create **visual elements**, including colors, typography, icons, and interactive components.

- Conduct **user research** and usability testing to validate design decisions.
- Collaborate with developers for accurate design implementation.
